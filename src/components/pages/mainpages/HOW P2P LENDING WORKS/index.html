HOW PEER-TO-PEER LENDING WORKS

Peer-to-peer Lending is a system that connects people seeking to invest money with borrowers looking for a loan. By cutting out the bank in the middle, investors earn a healthy return from principal and interest payments while helping others achieve their goals. This generally provides higher returns to the investors relative to other types of investments such as Mutual Funds, Banks etc and it helps to match up investors with borrowers looking for a personal loan, all of whom been vetted in advance for credit worthiness.

What are the benefits of peer-to-peer lending?

FOR THE INVESTOR

As an investor, when you lend money P2P, you can earn income on the returns as the borrower repays you. Those interest rates can be a few percentage points higher than what you might earn by keeping your money in a savings account.

FOR THE BORROWER

Benefits to the borrower revolve around speed of the funding, reasonable interest rates, higher funding rates, and the ease of the application process. Because of the P2P lending system, borrowers can easily access speedily receive the funds they need. Unlike in an average bank loan application that usually requires borrowers to wait several months!

FAQs ABOUT PEER-TO-PEER LENDING

Who can borrow on a P2P lending platform?

Anyone above 18 years of age, and registered with a valid bank account can borrow on the platform

How does P2P lending benefit investors?

P2P lending helps investors earn interest on the idle money which otherwise would not earn anything.

Do borrowers need to provide collaterals to access funds??

In most cases, borrowers do not need to provide collaterals. However, they do need to pass a verification process before they can be allowed to access funds
